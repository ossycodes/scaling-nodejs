As our database grows we will need to scale our database the x-axis of the scale cube is about scaling the database (horizontal partitioning)
horizontal partitioning or sharding involves adding more database instances that are each designed to store and provide access to a "part" of the 
database, each database is called a "shard". Each database is called a "shard" , there are many ways in which your database can be horizontally 
partitoned, you can horizontally partiton your database by the alphabet, region/zone, random identifiers etc
